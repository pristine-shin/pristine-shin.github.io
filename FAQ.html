FAQ

Hi! I'm Pristine. I am software engineer with over 10 years of experience in math education. I bring strong problem-solving, adaptability, collaboration skills, and an eye for aesthetic balance and design to full-stack development. ​Seeking new challenges and opportunities for growth, I completed App Academy’s rigorous full-stack program where I mastered technologies like JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Flask, and PostgreSQL. With my background in education and a commitment to continuous learning, I am eager to contribute to a collaborative team and build solutions that make a difference.

Can you say more about your background in education​?

I have extensive experience as a test prep coach, academic tutor, and scholarship coordinator at Study Smarter. I possess excellent interpersonal and written and spoken communication skills, and am able to connect and collaborate with many different types of people. 

As a high school math teacher, I have piloted novel curriculums from Geometry to AP Calculus, developed effective and differentiated lesson plans, and created a classroom culture of collaboration and growth mindset. These experiences have fostered my natural ability to lead, as well as my passion for learning and gaining hands-on experience. I am a quick learner, innovative, detail-oriented, and I thrive in a collaborative work/learning space.

Why are you transitioning careers?

After dedicating over a decade to education and assisting my students in reaching their full potential, I came to the realization that I had not afforded myself the same opportunities for growth and development over the course of my academic and professional journey. 

As fulfilling as teaching is, a large part of the joy for me is DOING the math itself, exercising those analytical and creative parts of my brain and being back in the learner’s seat. I decided to take a year off of full time teaching to explore other career options that continue my own learning. ​One of my best friends is a Principal Engineer and has been in the industry for over 10 years. Upon hearing about my desire for career exploration, they immediately said I should consider becoming a software engineer due to my exceptional communication skills, detail-orientation, analytical mind and problem solving disposition. 

Needless to say they were right. This past year has been deeply gratifying, transformative, and engaging, and I look forward to continuing to learn and grow in this next chapter of my professional life.

What grounds you and or brings your joy?

  • Cooking out of my pantry like it's a Chopped Challenge
  • Being outside in nature.
  • Woodworking and digital fabrication.
  • Chosen family and community.
